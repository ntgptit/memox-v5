Mx Design Principles

  • 1 Learning first, low cognitive load
  • 2 Dark mode optimized, light mode complete
  • 3 Readable long Korean + Vietnamese text
  • 4 Consistent interaction states
  • 5 No decorative complexity
  • 6 No hidden destructive actions
  • 7 No fake progress or placeholder-as-real data
  • 8 Every state is explicit
  • 9 Selected ≠ correct; wrong ≠ destructive
  • 10 Due ≠ wrong; no-due is not an error
  • 11 Colour is never the only signal
  • 12 Icon-only critical actions need a label
  • 13 Local-first safety over visual cleverness
